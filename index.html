About

Hello, I'm Jose, welcome to my website!
I'm an enthusiastic Data Scientist who is passionate about his job. From early ages, I always found interest in computers and later, in the data analysis. That's why I studied computer engineering at the University of Sevilla and a Master degree in Big Data Analytics at the Polytechnic University of Valencia. I love challenges and I’m always seeking new adventures and avid for new knowledge.

I currently work for an AI company where we apply machine learning, deep learning and data analysis techniques to solve different problems that arise in business. Apart from this, I also have experience in other related tasks such as the data anonymization, cleaning, pre-processing and everything related to this. Thanks to the experience I gained in my previous jobs and my education, I can help companies to get insight from their data and build predictive models to push their business forward.

Before this, I also worked as a Web Developer, and as a Researcher/Data Scientist in a Health Research Center, where I had the opportunity to apply statistics and different Machine Learning models to the cardiovascular research field, publishing some papers in related international journals.
